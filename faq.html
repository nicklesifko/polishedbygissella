Frequently Asked Questions.

Everything you need to know before booking your appointment.

Booking & Appointments

How do I book an appointment?

+

You can book directly through the Booking page on this site, or reach out via Instagram DM at @PolishedByGissella. All appointments are confirmed once a deposit is received.

How far in advance should I book?

+

Booking at least 5–7 days in advance is recommended, especially on weekends. Last-minute appointments are occasionally available — DM on Instagram to check availability.

Is a deposit required?

+

Yes, a $10 deposit is required to secure your appointment, particularly for new clients. The deposit amount goes toward your total service cost. Deposits are non-refundable if you cancel within 24 hours of your appointment.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Please give at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the appointment may result in a forfeited deposit. No-shows will be required to pay in full before booking again.

Where are you located?

+

Appointments take place in Round Rock, TX. The exact address will be shared once your appointment is confirmed.

Services & Pricing

How long does a typical appointment take?

+

A full set typically takes 1.5–2.5 hours depending on the style and complexity. Art or bling sets may take longer. Refills are usually 1–1.5 hours.

What does the "+" mean next to the prices?

+

The listed prices are starting prices for the base service. Add-ons like nail art, chrome, bling, length upgrades, or additional design complexity will increase the final price. You'll always be informed of the total before the service begins.

Can you remove nails done by another technician?

+

Yes! The Foreign Removal & Full Set service covers removal of a previous technician's work followed by a fresh full set, starting at $45+.

How many refill appointments can I get per set?

+

A maximum of 2 refill bookings are allowed per set for Hard Gel and Soft Builder Gel. After that, a full soak-off and new set is recommended to maintain the health and appearance of your nails.

Nail Care

How should I prepare for my appointment?

+

Come with clean, bare nails if possible. Remove any old polish beforehand. Avoid lotions or oils on your hands the day of your appointment, as this can affect adhesion. It also helps to come with inspo photos of the shape and style you want!

How long will my nails last?

+

With proper care, Gel X and Hard Gel sets typically last 3–5 weeks. Soft Builder Gel on natural nails can last 2–4 weeks. Results vary based on your nail growth, lifestyle, and aftercare habits.

Do you work on natural nails?

+

Yes! Soft Builder Gel and Hard Gel can both be applied directly on natural nails without extensions.
